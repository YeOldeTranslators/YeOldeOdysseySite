Ye Olde Odyssey

What is Ye Olde Odyssey?

Ye Olde Odyssey is a mod for the game "Super Mario Odyssey" by Nintendo, inspired by an AI Translator that takes English and translates it to "Ye Olde English" we realized that it might be funny to play Mario Odyssey with Everyone talking like they were in the 1700s. This mod is not aimed at being accurate and is instead aimed at being funny and entertaining.
